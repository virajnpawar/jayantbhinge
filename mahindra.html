Mahindra and Mahindra

Mahindra and Mahindra

We have proudly partnered with Mahindra & Mahindra since 2006, successfully executing over 35 industrial projects valued at ₹85 crores across various facilities in Nashik. Notable among them is the Scorpio TCF reconstruction featuring high-rise sheds and a suspended mezzanine floor, completed in 2012–13 with a project cost of ₹5 crores. Another significant highlight is the ASRS project, a first-of-its-kind automated storage rack system mounted on an RCC slab at the first-floor level.

Key facilities at Mahindra Plant II include a 1.7 lakh sq. ft. RCC warehouse, an advanced tool room with gantry provisions, and several plant expansions from 2006 to 2015. Our contributions extend to Mahindra CIE (MUSCO) with a massive steel press shop of 9500 sqm and 1100 tons of steel. Projects at Mahindra Intertrade Ltd. and multiple phases at Plant I further demonstrate our expertise in large-scale industrial infrastructure with consistent innovation and timely delivery.

1. The Scorpio TCF reconstruction

The Scorpio TCF reconstruction involved two large industrial sheds, including a 20-meter-high structure for an automated storage rack system and a suspended mezzanine floor, executed with precision to support advanced manufacturing operations at Mahindra Plant I, Nashik.

Project Name: Scorpio TCF Reconstruction (2012–13)
Area/Features: 20m-high shed with mezzanine for ASRS
Location: Mahindra Plant I, Satpur, Nashik
Cost: ₹5 Crores

2. Mahindra ASRS

The Mahindra ASRS project included a G+1 automated storage rack system measuring 67m x 7.5m x 24m high, uniquely designed with the entire rack system mounted on an RCC slab at the first-floor level—a first-time execution.

Project Name: Mahindra ASRS
Area: 67m x 7.5m x 24m high rack system.
Features: unique RCC slab-mounted design on the first floor

3. Mahindra Plant II

Mahindra Plant II in Satpur, Nashik, includes a 1.7 lakh sq. ft. RCC warehouse for CKD, a 90m x 35m tool room, and a die shop extension featuring a 32-ton gantry, executed across multiple phases from 2006 to 2015.

Project Name: Mahindra Plant II (2006–2015)
Area/Features: 1.7 lac sq ft RCC warehouse; 90m x 35m tool room; 32-ton gantry shed
Location: Satpur, Nashik
Architect: Ar. Dhananjay Patil

4. Mahindra CIE (MUSCO)

The Mahindra CIE (formerly MUSCO) project involved constructing a large press shop using 1100 tons of structural steel over a 9500 sqm area at MIDC, Nashik, delivered between 1997–99 with a project cost of ₹9 crores.

Project Name: Mahindra CIE (MUSCO) (1997–99)
Area/Features: 9500 sqm; 1100 tons of structural steel
Location: MIDC, Nashik
Cost: ₹9 Crores
Architect: Ar. Dhananjay Patil

5. Mahindra Intertrade Ltd.

The Mahindra Intertrade Ltd. project featured a 200m x 30m industrial shed with a 32-meter gantry, covering 6000 sqm for a blanking line facility, successfully executed in 2006–07 at Gonde, Nashik, under the guidance of Ar. Dhananjay Patil.

Project Name: Mahindra Intertrade Ltd. (2006–07)
Area/Features: 200m x 30m shed with 32m gantry; total area 6000 sqm
Location: Gonde, Nashik
Architect: Ar. Dhananjay Patil

6. Mahindra Plant I – Misc. Works

Mahindra Plant I – Misc. Works included the Verito Body Shop shift to UV line, FDS setup, low-height sheds, and projects U321, S201, and W501, executed between 2015–2019 with a combined project cost of ₹8 crores in Nashik.

Project Name: Mahindra Plant I – Misc. Works (2015–2019)
Location: Nashik
Cost: ₹8 Crores

7. W501 & W502 Projects – Mahindra Plant I

Executed additions and alterations at Thar Body Shop including machine and robot foundations, structural steel superstructures, pits, trenches, and supports for welding systems, electrical cabling, pipelines, and catwalks with integrated safety railings.

Project Name: W501 & W502 Projects – Mahindra Plant I (2021, 2024)
Location: Mahindra Plant I, Satpur, Nashik
Area: Machine & Robot Foundations, Structural Steel Superstructure
Cost: ₹10.10 Cr.
Architect: Jayant Bhinge
Structural Consultant: Jayant Bhinge
