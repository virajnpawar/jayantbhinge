Sula Vineyards Pvt. Ltd.

Sula Vineyards Pvt. Ltd.

Since 1995, Jayant Bhinge Consulting Engineers has partnered with Sula Vineyards Pvt. Ltd., executing over 30 projects across India, with a cumulative value of ₹65 crores. Notable works include the Bottle Storage Facilities I, II, and III in Nashik between 2004–2008 and a Winery Extension covering 1500 sqm completed in 2008. In 2007, we delivered Tank Foundations, ETP, and allied works at Jaulke, Wani. A significant milestone was the Bonded Warehouse and Office Building (39m x 47m, B+G+1) completed in 2011–12. In 2015–16, we executed the RMS & FGS Storage Building at Domaine Dindori, Jaulke, with a budget of ₹5 crores. Our scope expanded with the ₹6 crore Sula Resort Development at Govardhan, Nashik (2016–17) and the ₹3 crore Heritage Winery in Bangalore (2017–18). Most recently, we completed a Bottle & Gift Shop in Ramanagara, Bangalore, designed by Ar. Vibhavari Bhinge with structural consultancy by Jayant Bhinge.
