The Source at Sula

The Source at Sula is a distinctive boutique hotel project for Sula Vineyards Pvt. Ltd., reflecting creative reuse and architectural elegance. Originally an old factory and administrative setup, it was transformed into a 23-room luxury hotel featuring a spa, conference hall, swimming pool, patios, and dining spaces—each room uniquely designed in size and aesthetics. The project, completed in 2016–17 at a cost of ₹7 crores, stands as a landmark in adaptive reuse architecture. An 8-room extension followed in 2018, converting an industrial storage unit for ₹1.56 crores. In 2019–20, further expansion worth ₹9.4 crores was undertaken based on DSP Architects' concept, where we provided architectural drawings, tendering, and site monitoring. Other highlights include the Hermitage Suite—a luxury suite developed from a former games room (₹18 lakhs), a Tree House crafted with MS structure and wood cladding (₹1.5 crores), and Building 7, a 10-room facility with gym and games room (₹3 crores).

1. The Source

Converted an old factory and admin block into a 23-room boutique hotel with unique room designs, modern hospitality features, and landscaped courtyards, blending heritage with luxury for an elevated vineyard resort experience.

Project Name: The Source (2016–17)
Location: Sula Vineyards, Nashik
Area: 23 rooms, Spa, Pool, Dining
Cost: ₹7 Cr

2. Extension – 8 Room Block

Transformed an unused industrial storage structure into a standalone 8-room block, adding to the resort’s accommodation capacity while maintaining the rustic elegance and architectural consistency of The Source.

Project Name: Extension – 8 Room Block (2018)
Location: Sula Vineyards, Nashik
Area: 8 rooms
Cost: ₹1.56 Cr

3. Extension – DSP Architects

Based on DSP Architects' concept, we provided complete architectural drawings, structural design, tendering, and site supervision to expand The Source, seamlessly integrating new hospitality blocks within the existing resort.

Project Name: Extension – DSP Architects (2019–20)
Location: Sula Vineyards, Nashik
Cost: ₹9.4 Cr
Architect: DSP Architects, Mumbai
Structural Consultant: Jayant Bhinge

4. Hermitage Suite

Repurposed a games room into a luxurious Hermitage Suite, enhancing the guest experience with intimate and elevated hospitality offerings within the existing resort structure.

Project Name: Hermitage Suite
Location: Sula Vineyards, Nashik
Area: Premium Suite
Cost: ₹18 Lakhs

5. Tree House

Developed a bespoke tree house using MS structural framing and wooden cladding, offering a unique elevated stay experience within the vineyard, merging nature with architecture.

Project Name: Tree House
Location: Sula Vineyards, Nashik
Area: Tree House, MS + Wooden Cladding
Cost: ₹1.5 Cr
Structural Consultant: Jayant Bhinge

6. The Source – Building 7

An old storage shed was converted into Building 7, housing 10 rooms, a gym, and games room—designed to enhance the resort’s accommodation and recreation capacity.

Project Name: The Source – Building 7 (2021–22)
Location: Sula Vineyards, Nashik
Area: 10 rooms | Gym | Games Room | 43m x 15m
Cost: ₹3 Cr
Structural Consultant: Jayant Bhinge
