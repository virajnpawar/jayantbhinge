Institutional Projects

Institutional Projects

Jayant Bhinge Consulting Engineers has played a pivotal role in delivering high-quality structural solutions for prominent institutional campuses. At Mumbai Education Trust (MET), we were instrumental in constructing the G+5 Engineering Building (2005–07), covering administration, pharmacy, management, and workshop blocks. The campus later saw an extension to the Pharmacy building (2015–16) and a major expansion to the Engineering and MBA buildings in 2025, covering 78,000 sq.ft at a project cost of ₹14 Cr, with structural consultancy led by Jayant Bhinge.


In 1997–98, we provided structural solutions for the Mahindra Institute of Quality (MMDC) at Village Govardhan, Nashik—a flagship learning facility by Mahindra Group, designed by Environ Planners.


From 2015–17, we collaborated on the Wisdom High School in Govardhan, Nashik—a ₹12 Cr project designed by Ar. Satish Madhivala—delivering a modern, efficient educational infrastructure known for its blend of design and durability.
