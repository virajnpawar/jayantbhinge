TDK - EPCOS

TDK - EPCOS Satpur, Nashik, has been our esteemed partner since 1997, during which we have executed over fifteen projects valued at approximately ₹180 crores. Our portfolio includes multiple phases for Electronic Hi-Tech Components Ltd. (EPCOS), beginning with a 6,500 sq.m facility completed between 1997 and 2001 at an estimated cost of ₹28 crores, followed by EPCOS India Pvt. Ltd. Phase III in 2004 and Phase IV in 2007-08, and a cutting-edge factory building in 2016-17. In addition, we delivered TDK India Pvt. Ltd.'s DC building sheeting and miscellaneous work at E-22 to E-25 in Nashik for ₹1.50 crores, exemplifying meticulous project execution. Our latest accomplishment, the New Factory Building (Samruddhi Building) at E-25/2, spans 2,20,000 sq.ft and cost ₹77.0 crores, highlighting our innovation and commitment to superior design and construction. Each project demonstrates our unwavering dedication to quality, innovative engineering, and timely delivery, reinforcing our leadership in the industry.
